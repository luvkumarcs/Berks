Why Choose Us

Join our team

Here at Berks, our main focus is nurturing an environment of progress and advancement, where each person is respected and given the ability to thrive. We are dedicated to promoting the ongoing development of our team, inspiring them to strive for outstanding achievements and providing unwavering support throughout their journey. We aim to establish a setting where both personal and professional growth is not only endorsed but also commemorated, as individuals accomplish incredible milestones day by day.

What is on offer?

  • Competitive base salary and unlimited commissions.
  • Car fuel expense allowances.
  • medical insurance allowance ( after 90 days )
  • Full-time work schedule, Mon-Fri with weekends off!
  • Company-branded clothing and identification badge.
  • Paid initial training on products and services.
  • Continuous training for your professional development.
  • Abundant career advancement opportunities in the USA.

The Neighborhood Sales Agents are responsible for:

Engaging potential customers in various communities by using company-provided leads for potential customers and selling our client’s services.

Potential customers are contacted by:

Going door to door using leads in various communities (90%)

By attending events such as home shows, residential building events & community events, closing sales at the various events or following up on leads generated from such events (10%).

Skills Experience:

Ability to work independently.
Self-motivated.
Excellent communicator.
Ability to reach sales targets.
Ability to travel – this role will consist of 80% travel time.
